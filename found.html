Found

About the Show

*Found* is a gripping drama that centers around Gabi Mosely, a public relations specialist who leads a crisis management team dedicated to finding missing people — especially those the system has forgotten. The twist? She hides a dark secret about her own past.

Why I Love It

  • Focuses on real social issues
  • Strong, complex characters
  • Each episode brings a powerful emotional journey
  • The mystery keeps getting deeper

Favourite Character

Gabi Mosely – She's passionate, fearless, and driven to protect the vulnerable.
