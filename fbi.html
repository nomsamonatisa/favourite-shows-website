My Favourite Show: FBI

What is FBI About?

FBI is a thrilling American crime drama that follows the investigations of the New York field office of the Federal Bureau of Investigation. Each episode deals with major crimes like terrorism, organized crime, and high-stakes cases, all solved by a brave team of agents.

Why I Love FBI

  • It's full of action and suspense
  • The characters are smart, brave, and inspiring
  • I enjoy watching how they solve difficult cases
  • The teamwork and justice message are powerful

My Favourite Character

Maggie Bell – She’s confident, intelligent, and never gives up on justice!

Favourite Episode

The episode where the team uncovers a major bombing plot and stops it just in time. It was so intense!
