The Rookie

About the Show

The Rookie follows John Nolan, the oldest rookie in the LAPD, as he navigates the challenges of starting a new career in law enforcement while balancing family and personal growth.

Why I Love It

  • Unique perspective of a late career change
  • Engaging mix of drama, action, and humor
  • Relatable characters with real struggles
  • Strong focus on teamwork and personal growth

Favourite Character

John Nolan – Determined and optimistic, he proves it’s never too late to follow your dreams.
