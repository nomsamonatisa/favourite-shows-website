NCIS: Sydney

About the Show

NCIS: Sydney expands the NCIS universe to Australia, where a team of U.S. NCIS agents and Australian Federal Police work together to solve crimes involving the Navy and national security, blending American and Aussie styles of investigation.

Why I Love It

  • Fresh take on the classic NCIS formula
  • Beautiful Australian locations and culture
  • Cross-cultural teamwork and tensions
  • Intriguing cases with international stakes

Favourite Character

Michelle Mackey – A strong and fearless leader who brings sharp instincts and passion to every case.
