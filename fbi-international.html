FBI: International

About the Show

FBI: International follows the elite team of FBI agents who operate around the globe to protect Americans wherever they may be. They handle high-stakes investigations and navigate dangerous territories far from home.

Why I Love It

  • International missions and travel
  • High-tech investigations
  • Smart agents who work well as a team
  • The tension and drama of foreign cases

Favourite Character

Special Agent Scott Forrester – A determined leader who always puts his team and the mission first.
